PROBLEM STATEMENT
Portfolio managers in capital markets need concise, accurate risk summaries to make informed investment decisions.
Current risk reports are lengthy and complex, requiring manual interpretation of multiple data sources such as market data, holdings, and external news. This delays decision-making and increases the risk of missed exposures. The scale involves portfolios with hundreds of holdings and daily market data updates. Existing tools lack generative Al capabilities to synthesize and summarize risk information contextually. There is a need for an Al-powered summarization tool that produces clear, actionable portfolio risk insights.
Data Considerations:
Require portfolio holdings data, real-time market prices, news sentiment feeds, and historical risk metrics. Data formats include JSON, CSV, and XML from APls. Data volume includes daily updates for hundreds of securities.
Preprocessing involves data cleansing, normalization, and sentiment scoring. Privacy and compliance protocols must be observed for sensitive financial data. Synthetic data can simulate portfolio scenarios and market shocks for testing.
Consider using synthetic or anonymized data where appropriate. Ensure data quality and relevance to the problem context.
SOLUTION EXPECTATIONS
Deliver a web interface where portfolio managers view Al-generated daily risk summaries that synthesize multiple data sources in natural language. Features include interactive charts, alerts for significant risk changes, and export options.
Integrate with financial market and portfolio APls. Success metrics include 85%+ summary relevance and positive user feedback. Demonstrate by generating reports for sample portfolios and showing integration with live data feeds.
Provide user guide, technical documentation, and deployment instructions.
